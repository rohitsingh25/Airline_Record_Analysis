Graph Explanation

The 'Count of Flights Over Time Based on Flight Status' line plot illustrates the variation in the flight count over time with respect to various flight statuses. The number of flights is displayed on the y-axis, and the departure dates are represented on the x-axis. To quickly differentiate between different flight statuses, such as "On Time," "Cancelled," and "Delayed," the plot uses different colored lines. It is possible to observe temporal patterns and trends in flight statuses with this graphical representation. By examining this plot, one can gain understanding of how flight statuses are distributed over time and find when there are more or fewer on-time, cancelled, or delayed flights. The legend makes it easier to understand which flight status each line corresponds to, making the visualization educational for understanding the temporal dynamics of flight operations.
