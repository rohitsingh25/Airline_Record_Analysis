Graph Explanation

The distribution of passengers among the various countries and continents is shown hierarchically in the sunburst chart named "Airport Country and Continent-wise Passengers." With the outer layers representing continents and the inner layers highlighting individual countries, each level of the sunburst provides insightful information about passenger counts. Each segment's size and color intensity match the number of passengers, making it possible to compare the volume of air travel visually. Warmer tones in the color gradient indicate higher passenger volumes, improving clarity. This visualization offers a thorough overview of the dataset and a dynamic investigation of the distribution of passenger traffic across nations and continents, making it an effective tool for figuring out global aviation patterns and trends.
