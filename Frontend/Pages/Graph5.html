Graph Explanation

A count plot titled 'Count vs Age ' shows how passengers are distributed among different age groups. The age categories are represented by the x-axis, and the number or frequency of passengers in each age group is shown by the y-axis. Plt.figure(figsize=(18,10))'s figsize parameter guarantees a larger, easier-to-read plot. When working with a large number of age categories, it is more readable to rotate the x-axis labels by ninety degrees. The resulting graphic makes it easier to identify common age groups and possible outliers by giving a clear picture of how passengers are distributed across various age ranges.
